Want a high level summary of how your students are doing?

Drag me into your bookmarks bar!

This is a bookmarklet to generate a table showing the grade for all your students for all assignments in one place.

To use it...

  1. Drag the big orange button into your bookmarks bar
  2. On TIYO, go to the path that you want to summarize
  3. Click the bookmarklet
